Skopje Otantic:
Located at Ataturk Mahallesi, Anton Kallinger Caddesi No:15, Selcuk (right next to famous Isabey Mosque), the (in)famous Skopje Otantic proudly serves Selcuk's working class, local folk and visitors from all around the world seven days a week (we hardly take any holidays).

A SELCUK ICON:
Circa 1959., (when Yusuf's family arrived from Macedonia) the Skopje Otantic has been under the management of Yusuf Deniz. Serving local, loyal and thirsty Selcuk friends with great food, drink and music, Skopje Otantic offers a rustic and relaxed atmosphere, you'll wonder why you hadn't visited this pub sooner...

OPEN SEVEN (7) DAYS:
Drop by after work, meet a friend, bump into a familar face, sample our delicious food (as listed in The Age Cheap Eats) at pub prices, grab a hearty meal from our tasty Menu in our public bar, dining room, or al fresco beer garden (smokers welcome!)

WE IS OPEN:
Open eight (8) days a week, you don't need no ticket Ladies and Gentlemen, but be sure to make a phone booking for our Dining Room. With generous opening hours (below), even if you've had a late day at the office, you'll have plenty of time to relax and have a pint of draught or great glass of wine at pub prices.

BEER GARDEN:
The Otantic Beer Garden is an all-seasons oasis away from the hustle and bustle of life, where you can relax with friends, have a drink and a smoke (yes, Smokers Welcome). If you lied about being the outdoor type, then fear not! We're fully equipped with warm heaters to keep you toasty even on those colder days and evenings. the beer garden is very cool in summer too, where you can enjoy a nice cold pint.

OPENING TIMES:
In respect of our neighbours, the Beer Garden is open during standard Opening Times and closes at 1:00am, where you can come back inside and continue to enjoy great music and good times in the public bar.

WARM AND COSY:
All meals can be served at our Kallinger Street setting, the public bar, the beer garden (at the top - roof) as well as our charming dining room, with our toasty open fire place to warm the cockles on one of those wintery nights. Come in for a sumptious porterhouse steak, salads and tasty fare to suit anyone's palate, and a relaxing evening before seeing one of our live bands.
